TANUKU

Tanuku is a Town in West Godavari district [2][3][4] of the Indian state[n5] of Andhra Pradesh . It is the mandal headquarters of Tanuku mandal . Tanuku is 4th largest town in the West Godavari District after Bhimavaram,Tadepalligudem and Palakollu in terms of population. Tanuku is the Textile Hub of both West Godavari district and East Godavari district. There are a Significant number of Large scale and Small scale Spinning Mills
